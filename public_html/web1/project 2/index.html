Blub

- How Was I Created? -

Hello there! This is Blub!

Blub is a bubble from heaven! He has accidentally fallen from his home in the sky to Earth, and is trying to find his way back.

Blub was first created on Procreate, and then I vectorized him by bringing the Procreate image of him into Figma, lowering the image's opacity, tracing over the image using the pen tool, and filling in each path with its specific color!
